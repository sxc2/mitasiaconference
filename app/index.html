Asia Business Conference 2014

You are all cordially invited to MIT Sloan’s 4th annual Asia Business Conference. Come hear our esteemed guest speakers share their views on the roles that the current engines of Asian economic growth will continue to have in both the short and long-term. Speakers are experienced executives from various industries all with extensive experience in and working with Asian companies and/or governments. Please join us in listening to and discussing with our speakers on whether Asia’s growth will continue to be driven by these four engines. We also invite you to identify additional engines of growth that will contribute to the backbone of the emerging market that’s driving the world economy.
